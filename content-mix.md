## The Activation Post Strategy

An "activation post" is designed to generate comments from the right people (C-suite, board members, 
heads of strategy), after which Kate follows up personally via DM.

**What makes a good activation post for Kate's audience:**
- Challenges a belief that CEOs and board members hold with confidence
- Names a specific failure pattern they've likely experienced or fear
- Creates a clear agree/disagree dynamic without being inflammatory
- Examples:
  - "Most companies spending on AI this year will have nothing to show for it. Here's what separates the ones that won't."
  - "The Chief AI Officer role is being created for the wrong reasons. Here's when it's actually the right call."
  - "Your AI pilot succeeded. That's the problem."

**Follow-up DM template** (after someone comments meaningfully):
"[Name], your point about [specific thing they said] is exactly what I see with [type of organisation]. 
The gap between a successful pilot and real business impact is almost always an operating model problem, 
not a technology one. I run AI Opportunity Calls for exactly this situation — would you be open to a 
conversation?"

## Flagship Asset: AI ROI Playbook for CEOs

Kate's 12-month content strategy should build progressively toward this asset.

**Approach**: Every long-form article, deep insight, and major case study Kate publishes is either:
- A draft chapter or section of the Playbook
- A public teaser that creates demand for the full Playbook
- A proof point (OKKO, other cases) that will anchor the Playbook's credibility

**Content that feeds the Playbook:**
- Any post that defines a framework → candidate for a Playbook section
- Any case study with numbers → proof point for a Playbook chapter
- Any article that challenges a CEO-level assumption → could be a Playbook introduction

When generating content ideas, always flag if a topic has Playbook potential.

## Content-to-Conversion Path

Every piece of content fits somewhere on this path:

```
Awareness Post (provocation, insight — challenges CEO-level belief)
        ↓
Credibility Post (case study, results, speaking — proves Kate delivers)
        ↓
Intellectual Authority Post (operating model, governance, economics framework)
        ↓
Soft CTA ("I run AI Opportunity Calls for exactly this situation")
        ↓
Direct DM or comment follow-up
        ↓
AI Opportunity Call (free)
        ↓
AI Discovery proposal
```

The goal is that by the time Kate sends a DM, the person already thinks of her as the expert 
on this problem — not as someone selling a service. Content does the positioning; outreach does the conversion.
